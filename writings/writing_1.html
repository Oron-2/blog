But is it? Is this nothing but another utopia? Like the utopia of a world without evil? Who says what is evil and what is good? I know… many questions and not many answers. But one thing is for sure. For there to be good, there must be evil. How else will we be able to distinguish between the two? Even if something considered evil by some is considered good by others and vice versa, it means that both exist and are part of the universe. For one reason or another, there will always be wars, and they will be the field where good and evil meet, intertwine and fight.
